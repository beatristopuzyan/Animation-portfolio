About Me

Bringing Stories to Life, One Frame at a Time

Hi there! I’m Beatris Topuzyan, an animator and visual storyteller with a passion for transforming imagination into motion. Animation, to me, is more than just a craft — it’s a language, a way to communicate emotions, ideas, and stories that words alone can’t always express. From the first spark of inspiration to the final polished frame, I love every step of the creative process.

My work is rooted in a love for storytelling and character-driven narratives. Whether it’s a hand-drawn character sketch that evolves into a fully animated scene or a simple concept that grows into an immersive world, I’m drawn to projects that invite creativity, curiosity, and heart.

I work primarily in Procreate or on a piece of paper, and I’m always experimenting with new techniques, from traditional frame-by-frame animation to digital. The materials I use for traditional drawings include aquarels, acrylic markers, and alcoholic markers. Each project, whether personal or collaborative, is a chance to learn something new and push my creative boundaries a little further.

My inspiration comes from all around me — from the films and animated shorts I grew up watching, to the small everyday moments that spark an idea. I’m especially drawn to stories with a sense of whimsy and wonder, ones that blend imagination with a touch of reality, and characters that feel alive in their own unique ways.

Beyond animation, I love sketching ideas in my notebooks, building visual worlds through concept art, and diving into stories across different media — from comics and video games to films, books, and even the occasional spontaneous doodle on a napkin. Every piece of art, no matter how small, feeds into my creative process and helps me discover new ways to tell stories.

Thanks for visiting my portfolio and taking the time to learn a bit about me and my work. I hope my animations spark a sense of curiosity, joy, or wonder — and if you’re interested in collaborating, brainstorming ideas, or just geeking out about animation, I’d love to hear from you! ❤️
